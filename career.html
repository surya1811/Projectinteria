Why Become Interior designer?

Interia Team Skills

Spatial Awareness:
Understanding spaces and utilization while planning interiors, ambiance and room function.
Technical Knowledge:
To digitally create designs for clients, designers must be hands-on with software like Vectorworks, Autodesk Revit, 3D Max, Archicad, AutoCAD, Sketch Up, 3D Homeplanner, and Chief Architect.
Time Management:
Designers must know how to manage calendars, delegate, and manage time to get work done before deadlines.
Dynamic Creativity:
An interior designer must be creative and know artistic styles, colors, fabrics.
