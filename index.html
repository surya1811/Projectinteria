Interior Design

Interior design is an art and science of enhancing the interior of a building to achieve a healthier and more aesthetically pleasing environment for the people using the space. An interior designer plans, researches, coordinates, and manages such enhancement projects. It is a multifaceted profession that includes conceptual development, space planning , site inspections, programming, research, communicating with the stakeholders of a project, construction management, and execution of the design.

What We Do

We create interior environments that are functional, safe, and adhere to building codes, regulations and ADA requirements. We go beyond selection of color palettes and furnishings and apply our knowledge to the development of construction documents, occupancy loads, healthcare regulations and sustainable design principles, as well as the management and coordination of professional services including mechanical, electrical, plumbing, and life safety - all to ensure that people can live, learn or work in an innocuous environment that is also aesthetically pleasing. We make indoor spaces functional , safe and beautiful by determining space requirements and selecting essential and decorative items. We work with clients to create aesthetic rooms and spaces. The spaces we create are equally varied ranging from simple indoor and outdoor environments to hotel lobbies and lavish mansions. No matter the size , we work to create spaces that are attractive yet functional. We find new projects, and pinpointing the project goals, we sketch design plans. Then once we pick the materials and furnishings, we create project timelines estimating the costs , construction and installation. Coordinating with other professionals regarding plans and specifications, we ensure the client is satisfied after the project is complete.
